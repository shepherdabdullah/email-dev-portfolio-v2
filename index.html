About

I am a career oriented HTML Email Developer based in New Orleans, LA with practical skills in Email Design, Development, and Marketing. I am proficient in the hand coding and testing of HTML emails as well as working with frameworks especially MJML and prioritize UX including responsiveness and accessibility for all.

I strive for efficacy and possess a strong fundamental competence in the necessary and auxilliary tools of the trade ranging from development, design, automation, marketing, and version control applications.

As well as working effectively within teams and possessing strong communication skills I am comfortable with solitary assignments and have confidence in my decision making while holding a profound respect and appreciation for the opportunity to take the initiative.

I am eager to take on big challenges and am always looking to prove and improve myself in all that I do.
